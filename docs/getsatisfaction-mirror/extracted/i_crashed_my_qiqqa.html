I crashed my Qiqqa!

Hi,

I have been using Qiqqa, v.76, nearly every day for my Ph.D. work. Yesterday, after a few hours work, I found that the search function does not work at a library level. Search only works within pdf files.

So, I went to Qiqqa help and tried reindexing after selecting all pdfs several times, as suggested in one of the posts in the forum. Nothing happened. Tried logging out and restarting, uninstall and instal , v. 77. Not improvement at all. Tested out even simple words like 'technology' - 0 results except within a pdf. Even tried selecting all pdfs and launching search at a library level - 0 results again.
I even tried to start the indexing again from scratch and pressed 'rebuild search indices', and it crashed.
This is seriously not a good time for this to be happening to me. In the middle of working papers for publication and organising final draft of Ph.D.

Please HELP!!!

I`m sending the c:\temp\qiqqa.log file to support@qiqqa.com.
